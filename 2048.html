This is an implementation of Gabriele Cirulli's popular game "2048", written entirely from scratch by Kofi Doku Atuah for the Medullan recruitment's 2nd test. This application has not been tested in Internet Explorer

To play, first click on the grid to activate keyboard capture. Then use the arrow keys (up, down, left and right) to nudge the grid in the direction you want. The goal is to merge the numbers until you reach 2048.

Should you run out of grid-space before you hit 2048, you will fail the game.

Click the grid to deactivate keyboard-capture. Click it again to re-capture. Click it again to deactivate, and so on, ad infinitum. Click the "Reset" button below to reset the grid and try again.
