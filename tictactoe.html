This is an implementation of the very well-known game, Tic-Tac-Toe, written entirely from scratch by Kofi Doku Atuah for the Medullan recruitment's 2nd test. This application has not been tested in Internet Explorer

To play, first select whether you want to play versus the AI, or you want to play a two-player game. Then choose your desired difficulty. You can change these options anytime by selecting new options and clicking reset to apply the new options.

To reset the board after a game is done, click reset.

If the game is in single-player mode, sometimes the AI will play first and other times, the player will be expected to make the first move
